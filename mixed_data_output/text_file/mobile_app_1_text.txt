Q:How to view eStatement
Step 1: Please enter your 4-digit login PIN to proceed.
step 2: Click “Show Quick Balance” on the home page
step 3: Click on the available balance in the account.
Step 4: Click on the “View Statement” on the overview page
Step 5: It show the Account Statement
